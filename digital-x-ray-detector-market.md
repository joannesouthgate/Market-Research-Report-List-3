<p><p>Digital X-ray detectors are essential components in modern medical imaging technology. There are two main types of digital X-ray detectors: Wired and Wireless. Wired detectors require a physical connection to the imaging device, while wireless detectors use radio frequency signals to transfer images. Wired detectors are typically more reliable and have faster data transfer speeds, but can be less flexible in terms of positioning. Wireless detectors offer more freedom of movement and easier integration into existing systems, but may have limitations in terms of signal interference and distance.</p></p>
