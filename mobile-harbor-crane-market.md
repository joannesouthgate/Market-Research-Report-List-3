<p><p>Mobile Harbor Crane Market Types are categorized based on their design and structure. Gantry Cranes have a horizontal beam supported by legs that run on wheels along a track. Half Gantry Cranes have one side supported by legs while the other side is supported by a wall or structure. Double Cantilever Gantry Cranes have horizontal beams supported by legs on both ends with no additional support in the middle. Each type of crane offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications in harbor and port operations.</p></p>
