<p><p>Hindered Amine Stabilizers (HALS) are additives used in polymers to protect against degradation caused by UV radiation. They come in three main types: Polymeric HALS, Monomeric HALS, and Oligomeric HALS. Polymeric HALS are long-chain polymers that provide long-term protection, Monomeric HALS are low molecular weight compounds suitable for high-temperature processing, and Oligomeric HALS are intermediate in size and provide a balance between the other two types. Each type offers different benefits and is used in various applications based on the desired level of protection and processing requirements.</p></p>
