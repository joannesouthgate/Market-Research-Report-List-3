Lignite is the lowest quality coal with high moisture content, while Sub-Bituminous has a higher heat value but lower carbon content. Bituminous coal is widely used for electricity generation due to its high energy content. Anthracite is the highest quality coal with the highest carbon content and is mainly used for heating purposes. Each type caters to different industries and applications within the coal trading market.</p></p>
