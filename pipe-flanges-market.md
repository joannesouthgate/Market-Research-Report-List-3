<p><p>Pipe flanges are integral components in piping systems used to connect pipelines and valves. There are different types of pipe flanges in the market, including blind flanges, lap joint flanges, slip-on flanges, socket weld flanges, and threaded flanges. Blind flanges are used to seal the end of a pipe, lap joint flanges allow for easy alignment of bolt holes, slip-on flanges are easy to install, socket weld flanges provide a smooth bore for better flow, and threaded flanges allow for easy assembly and disassembly.</p></p>
