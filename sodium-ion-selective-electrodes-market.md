<p><p>Sodium Ion Selective Electrodes are available in two main types - Liquid Membrane and PVC Membrane. Liquid Membrane electrodes use a solvent-based membrane that selectively allows sodium ions to pass through, while PVC Membrane electrodes use a polyvinyl chloride membrane. Liquid Membrane electrodes are known for their high sensitivity and fast response time, while PVC Membrane electrodes are preferred for their ease of use and durability. Both types of electrodes are widely used in industries such as healthcare, environmental monitoring, and research.</p></p>
