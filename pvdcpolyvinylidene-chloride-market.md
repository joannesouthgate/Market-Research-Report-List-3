<p><p>PVDC (Polyvinylidene Chloride) is a versatile polymer used in various markets. PVDC Latex is a water-based emulsion of PVDC, commonly used as a barrier coating for packaging materials to provide moisture resistance and oxygen barrier properties. On the other hand, PVDC resins are solid forms of PVDC, suitable for applications requiring high barrier properties such as in food packaging films. Both PVDC Latex and PVDC resins cater to different market needs but share the common goal of providing excellent barrier protection.</p></p>
