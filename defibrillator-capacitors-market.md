<p><p>Defibrillator capacitors are used in medical devices to deliver an electric shock to the heart in cases of cardiac arrest. There are two main types of defibrillator capacitors on the market: monophasic and biphasic. Monophasic defibrillator capacitors deliver a single shock waveform, while biphasic defibrillator capacitors deliver a shock in two phases. Both types are designed to restore the heart's normal rhythm and are essential components of defibrillator devices used in emergency medical situations.</p></p>
