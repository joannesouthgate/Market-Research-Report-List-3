<p><ul><li>High Tension (HT)</li><li>Extra High Tension (EHT)</li><li>Ultra High Tension (UHT))</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power Transmission Lines & Towers Market Types include High Tension (HT), Extra High Tension (EHT), and Ultra High Tension (UHT). High Tension lines typically carry voltages between 60 kV and 150 kV, while Extra High Tension lines carry voltages between 150 kV and 400 kV. Ultra High Tension lines are designed to carry voltages above 400 kV. These different types of transmission lines are used to efficiently transmit electricity over long distances, ensuring a reliable supply of power to consumers.</p></p>
