<p><p>DBS devices for Parkinson's disease come in two primary market types: single-channel DBS and dual-channel DBS. Single-channel DBS systems involve one electrode placed in the brain to deliver electrical stimulation, while dual-channel DBS systems use two separate electrodes for more targeted and adjustable stimulation. Both types of devices aim to alleviate symptoms of Parkinson's disease by modulating abnormal brain activity. The choice between single-channel and dual-channel DBS depends on the specific needs and preferences of the patient and their healthcare provider.</p></p>
