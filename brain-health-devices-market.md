<p><p>Brain Health Devices Market Types include various technologies used for diagnosing and monitoring brain health. Electroencephalogram (EEG) measures electrical activity in the brain, Computed Tomography (CT) provides detailed images of the brain, Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) uses magnetic fields to produce detailed images, Magnetoencephalogram (MEG) measures magnetic fields produced by brain activity, Transcranial Doppler (TCD) measures blood flow in the brain, Intracranial Pressure (ICP) monitors pressure inside the skull, and Cerebral Oximeters measure oxygen levels in the brain. These devices play a crucial role in identifying brain disorders and guiding treatment decisions.</p></p>
