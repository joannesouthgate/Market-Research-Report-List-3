<p><p>Shaving lotions and creams can be divided into two main market types: aftershave and pre-shave products. Aftershave lotions are used post-shave to soothe the skin, minimize irritation, and provide hydration. They often contain ingredients like aloe vera, witch hazel, and menthol. On the other hand, pre-shave products are used before shaving to soften the hair, create a barrier between the skin and razor, and improve glide. They typically contain oils, glycerin, and emollients to prepare the skin for a smooth shave.</p></p>
