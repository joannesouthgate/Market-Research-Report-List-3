<p><p>Magnetic Fluid Seals for Semiconductor market types include Hollow Shaft Seals and Solid Shaft Seals. Hollow Shaft Seals are used in applications where there is a need to pass a shaft through the seal, such as in vacuum systems or rotating machinery. Solid Shaft Seals, on the other hand, are used in applications where the shaft does not need to pass through the seal, such as in pressure vessels or fixed machinery. Both types of seals utilize magnetic fluid to provide a reliable and efficient sealing solution in semiconductor applications.</p></p>
