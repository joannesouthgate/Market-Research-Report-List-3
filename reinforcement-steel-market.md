<p><p>Reinforcement steel market consists of two main types: deformed steel and mild steel. Mild steel, on the other hand, is a more flexible and easily welded type of reinforcement steel. Both types are widely used in the construction industry for strengthening concrete structures and ensuring their durability and stability.</p></p>
