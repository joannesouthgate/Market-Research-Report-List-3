<p><p>Optically clear glass is a type of glass that provides high transparency, clarity, and minimal distortion. Two main types of optically clear glass markets are float glass and rolled glass. Float glass is made through a process where molten glass is floated on molten tin, resulting in a smooth surface with high optical quality. Rolled glass, on the other hand, is produced by passing molten glass between two rollers to create a smooth surface. Both float glass and rolled glass are widely used in applications where high transparency and clarity are required.</p></p>
