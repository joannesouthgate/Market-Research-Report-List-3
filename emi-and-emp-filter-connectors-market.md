The market types include C Circuit Filter, L-C Circuit Filter, Pi Circuit Filter, and others. C Circuit Filters use capacitors to block high-frequency noise, L-C Circuit Filters use inductors and capacitors to reduce noise across a wider frequency range, while Pi Circuit Filters add an additional capacitor to further suppress noise. Other filter types may include custom designs or variations of the aforementioned circuits.</p></p>
