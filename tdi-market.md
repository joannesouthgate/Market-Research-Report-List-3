<p><p>2,4-TDI (CAS: 584-84-9) and 2,6-TDI (CAS: 91-08-7) are two isomers of TDI used in the production of polyurethanes. 2,4-TDI is the more commonly used isomer, known for its high reactivity and excellent chemical resistance. On the other hand, 2,6-TDI is less reactive and offers better heat resistance properties. Both isomers have their own market demand based on specific application requirements, with 2,4-TDI generally preferred in flexible foam production and 2,6-TDI in rigid foam applications.</p></p>
