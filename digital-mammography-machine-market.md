<p><strong>The Digital Mammography Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Digital Mammography (FFDM)</li><li>Digital Breast Tomography (DBT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Full Digital Mammography (FFDM) machines are used for breast cancer screening. They create detailed digital images of the breast for analysis by radiologists, providing a more accurate and efficient method compared to traditional film mammography. On the other hand, Digital Breast Tomography (DBT) machines use specialized technology to capture multiple images of the breast from different angles, allowing for a 3D reconstruction. This advanced imaging technique is particularly useful for detecting abnormalities in dense breast tissue. Both FFDM and DBT machines play a crucial role in early breast cancer detection and diagnosis.</p></p>
