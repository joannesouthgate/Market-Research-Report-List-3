<p><p>The Mechanical Tyre Vulcanizer market can be categorized into three types: AFV (Automatic Fully-Vulcanized), BOM (Built-On Machine), and AVBO (Automatic Vulcanization Built On). AFV systems automatically fully vulcanize a tyre, while BOM systems require a separate machine for vulcanization. AVBO systems combine automatic vulcanization with built-on features for added efficiency. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of speed, ease of use, and overall performance in the tyre vulcanization process.</p></p>
