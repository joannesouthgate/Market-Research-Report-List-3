<p><p>Real-time location service market can be categorized into two main types: tags or signal transmitters and Wi-Fi networks. Tags or signal transmitters are physical devices that can be attached to objects or personnel to track their location in real-time using technologies such as GPS or RFID. On the other hand, Wi-Fi networks utilize existing wireless networks to track the location of devices or individuals within a specified area. Both types of technologies play a crucial role in enabling accurate real-time tracking and monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
