<p><p>Single flow air conditionings can be classified into two main types based on their installation orientation: horizontal type and vertical type. Horizontal type air conditioners are typically mounted close to the ceiling and are ideal for cooling large spaces with a uniform airflow distribution. On the other hand, vertical type air conditioners are usually installed closer to the floor and are more suitable for smaller rooms or areas where the airflow needs to be directed upwards.
