<p><p>Polysilicon rod market comprises of two main types: Silane Method and Metallurgical Process. Silane Method involves producing high-quality polysilicon rods through the chemical reaction of silane gas, while Metallurgical Process involves refining metallurgical-grade silicon to obtain high-purity polysilicon rods. Silane Method is considered more cost-effective and environmentally friendly, while Metallurgical Process is known for its high productivity and lower energy consumption. Both methods cater to the increasing demand for polysilicon rods in various industries such as semiconductor and solar energy.</p></p>
