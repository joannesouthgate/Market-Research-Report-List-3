<p><p>Advanced glazing material market offers a variety of types to cater to different needs. Absorbing glass absorbs a portion of sunlight, reducing glare and heat. Dark glass provides privacy and reduces visible light transmission. Reflective glass reflects sunlight and heat, reducing energy consumption.
