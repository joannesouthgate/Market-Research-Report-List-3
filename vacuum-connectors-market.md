Ultra-High Vacuum (UHV) connectors are used in applications that require vacuum levels up to 10^-9 Torr. Extreme-High Vacuum (XHV) connectors are used in applications that require vacuum levels below 10^-9 Torr. Each type of connector is designed to maintain the required vacuum levels for specific applications in industries such as aerospace, semiconductor, and research.</p></p>
