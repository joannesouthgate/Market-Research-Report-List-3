<p><p>In-vehicle networking is crucial for communication within a vehicle's systems. Different types of networks serve various purposes. Controller Area Network (CAN) is widely used for real-time communication between electronic control units. Local Interconnect Network (LIN) is used for lower speed, cost-effective applications like window control systems. FlexRay offers high speed and reliability for advanced safety systems. Ethernet is becoming more popular for higher bandwidth requirements like infotainment systems. Each type of network plays a specific role in ensuring efficient and reliable communication within a vehicle.</p></p>
