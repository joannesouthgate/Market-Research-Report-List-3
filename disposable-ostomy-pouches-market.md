<p><p>Disposable Ostomy Pouches come in two main types - Drainable Ostomy Pouches and Closed Ostomy Pouches. Drainable pouches have an opening at the bottom that allows for easy emptying of waste. They are ideal for individuals with colostomies who have predictable bowel movements. Closed pouches, on the other hand, do not have an opening and are discarded when full. They are suitable for those with ileostomies or urostomies who have more frequent output. Both types offer convenience and discretion for ostomy patients.</p></p>
