<p><p>The Next-Generation Multi-purpose Biosensors market includes various types such as Nanomechanical Biosensors, Optical Biosensors, Electrochemical Biosensors, Thermistor/Calorimetric Biosensors, and other biosensors. Nanomechanical biosensors utilize nanoscale mechanical resonators for sensing applications, while optical biosensors detect changes in light properties to analyze biological samples. Electrochemical biosensors measure electrical signals produced by interactions between biological molecules and electrodes. Thermistor/Calorimetric biosensors measure changes in temperature or heat flow during biochemical reactions. Other biosensors may include acoustic wave, piezoelectric, and magnetic field biosensors.</p></p>
