<p><p>Small Animal Imaging Market includes three main types - Micro-MRI, Optical, and Nuclear imaging. Micro-MRI uses magnetic resonance imaging to visualize the internal structures of small animals. Optical imaging involves the use of light to create images of biological structures. Nuclear imaging utilizes radioactive tracers to produce images of physiological processes within animals. Each type of imaging offers unique advantages and applications in preclinical research, allowing researchers to study disease progression, treatment efficacy, and underlying biological mechanisms in small animal models.</p></p>
