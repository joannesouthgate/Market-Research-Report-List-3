<p><p>Lab heating and drying ovens are necessary equipment in various industries for drying, curing, sterilizing, and heat treating applications. The two main types of lab ovens are forced air circulation ovens and gravity convection ovens. Forced air circulation ovens use a fan to evenly distribute heat throughout the chamber, providing uniform temperature control. Gravity convection ovens rely on natural convection currents for heat distribution, making them more suitable for applications where delicate materials may be affected by forced air.</p></p>
