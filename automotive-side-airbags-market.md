<p><p>Automotive side airbags come in two main types: Side Torso Airbags and Side Tubular or Curtain Airbags. Side Torso Airbags are typically located in the seat and deploy from the side of the vehicle to protect the torso and chest of the occupants in the event of a side collision. On the other hand, Side Tubular or Curtain Airbags deploy from the roof or door frame and provide protection to the head and neck of the occupants. Both types of side airbags are designed to enhance the safety of vehicle occupants during side impact collisions.</p></p>
