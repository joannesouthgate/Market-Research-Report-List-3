<p><p>A laboratory colorimeter is a device used to measure the concentration of a particular substance in a liquid or solution based on its color intensity. There are two main types of laboratory colorimeters: handheld and benchtop. Handheld colorimeters are portable, lightweight devices that are convenient for field or on-site testing. Benchtop colorimeters are larger, stationary units typically used in laboratory settings for more accurate and precise measurements. Both types of colorimeters serve the same purpose of quantifying color intensity for various applications in research, quality control, and scientific analysis.</p></p>
