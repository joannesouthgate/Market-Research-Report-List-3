<p><p>Geophysical Processing & Imaging market includes various types of technologies such as Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR), Electromagnetic methods, LIDAR, and Others. GPR is used for subsurface imaging through radar pulses, while Electromagnetic methods measure changes in the Earth's magnetic field. LIDAR uses laser pulses to create detailed 3D images of the earth's surface. Other techniques include gravity surveys, seismic methods, and magnetotellurics. Each type of technology offers unique advantages for imaging and processing geophysical data for various applications.</p></p>
