<p><p>High frequency surface mount inductors are available in three main types. Wire wound type inductors are made of a coil of wire wrapped around a core, providing high inductance values. Film type inductors use a thin film of conductive material to create the coil, allowing for higher frequencies and lower losses. Multilayer type inductors use layers of conductive material separated by insulating layers, providing compact size and high inductance values. Each type caters to different applications and requirements in the high frequency surface mount inductors market.</p></p>
