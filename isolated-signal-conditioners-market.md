<p><p>Isolated signal conditioners are devices used to amplify, filter, convert, or isolate electrical signals in various industrial applications. The market for these devices can be categorized into three main types: single channel, dual channel, and multi-channel. Single channel signal conditioners are designed to handle one input signal at a time, while dual channel signal conditioners can handle two input signals simultaneously. Multi-channel signal conditioners have the ability to handle multiple input signals at once, making them suitable for more complex systems that require monitoring and control of several signals.</p></p>
