<p><p>Vertical metal balers are commonly classified into two types: manual and PLC automatic. Manual vertical balers require the operator to manually load and operate the machine, making them suitable for smaller-scale operations. On the other hand, PLC automatic vertical metal balers are equipped with programmable logic controllers that automate the baling process, making them more efficient and suitable for larger industrial settings. Both types of vertical metal balers play a crucial role in recycling and waste management industries.</p></p>
