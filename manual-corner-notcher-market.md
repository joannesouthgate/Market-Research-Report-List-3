<p><p>Manual corner notchers come in two main market types: Fixed Angle and Variable Angle. Fixed Angle notchers have a predetermined cutting angle that cannot be adjusted, making them simpler to use but less versatile. On the other hand, Variable Angle notchers allow the user to adjust the cutting angle according to their specific requirements, providing greater flexibility for different types of notching tasks. Both types cater to different user preferences and are used in various industries for cutting and shaping metal sheets.</p></p>
