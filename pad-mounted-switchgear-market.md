<p><p>Pad-mounted switchgear refers to electrical equipment used to control power distribution in outdoor settings. There are two main types of pad-mounted switchgear: gas insulated and air insulated. Gas insulated switchgear uses sulfur hexafluoride gas to insulate the components, while air insulated switchgear uses air as the insulation medium. Gas insulated switchgear is preferred for its compact size and higher reliability, while air insulated switchgear is simpler and often more cost-effective. Both types are used in various applications for distributing electricity safely and efficiently.</p></p>
