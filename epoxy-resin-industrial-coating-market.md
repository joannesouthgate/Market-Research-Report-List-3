<p><strong>The Epoxy Resin Industrial Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Type</li><li>Waterborne Type</li><li>Non Solvent Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Epoxy resin industrial coatings can be categorized into three main types based on their solvent composition: solvent type, waterborne type, and non-solvent type. Solvent type coatings use volatile organic compounds as solvents, waterborne type coatings use water as the main solvent, and non-solvent type coatings do not use any solvent. Each type of coating offers different benefits and is suitable for specific applications, with waterborne coatings being more environmentally friendly and non-solvent coatings offering low odor and VOC emissions.</p></p>
