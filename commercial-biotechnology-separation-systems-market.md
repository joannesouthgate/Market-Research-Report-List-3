Membrane Filtration is used for separation based on molecule size. Liquid Chromatography separates compounds based on their interaction with a stationary phase. Centrifuge separates particles based on density. Electrophoresis separates molecules based on their charge and size. Flow Cytometry is used for cell analysis based on their characteristics. These systems play a crucial role in biotechnology processes for separation and analysis of biomolecules.</p></p>
