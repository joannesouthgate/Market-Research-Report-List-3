<p><p>Open gate hot runner is a type of hot runner system used in injection molding processes to deliver molten plastic material into a mold cavity. The open gate hot runner system is preferred by manufacturers for its simplicity, easy maintenance, and flexibility in handling a wide range of thermoplastics.</p><p>The Open Gate Hot Runner Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.6% during the forecast period.
