<p><p>Low-frequency sound-absorbing insulation material market types include closed-cell foams and open-cell foams. Closed-cell foams are dense and rigid, providing excellent thermal insulation and structural support. They are preferred for applications requiring high strength and durability. Open-cell foams, on the other hand, are softer and more flexible, with a higher sound-absorbing capacity. They are commonly used in acoustic panels and soundproofing applications where flexibility and conformability are needed. Both types offer effective sound absorption properties, catering to different needs in the market.</p></p>
