<p><p>Solar Cells and Modules Market Types include Mono-Si Modules, Multi-Si Modules, CdTe Modules, CIGS Modules, A-Si Modules, and Others. Mono-Si Modules are made from single-crystal silicon, offering high efficiency. Multi-Si Modules are made from polycrystalline silicon, providing lower efficiency but lower cost. CdTe Modules use cadmium telluride as a semiconductor. CIGS Modules use copper, indium, gallium, and selenium to create thin-film solar cells. A-Si Modules use amorphous silicon for flexibility. Other types include organic photovoltaic cells and dye-sensitized solar cells.</p></p>
