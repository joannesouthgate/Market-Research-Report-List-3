<p><p>Non-Venting Level Sensors are used to measure the liquid level in closed containers without allowing the exchange of gases between the sensor and the container. There are two main types of Non-Venting Level Sensors - Float Type and Hydrostatic. </p><p>Float Type Sensors use a float that rises and falls with the liquid level, while Hydrostatic Sensors measure the pressure exerted by the liquid in the container to determine the level. Both types are commonly used in industrial applications where accurate and reliable liquid level measurement is critical.</p></p>
