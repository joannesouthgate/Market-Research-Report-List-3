<p><p>Automated Border Control Solution market comprises two main types: ABC E-gate and ABC Kiosk. ABC E-gates are automated gates that use biometric technology to verify travelers' identities and passports, allowing for quick and efficient border crossing. On the other hand, ABC Kiosks are self-service machines where travelers can authenticate their identities using biometric data and answer questions electronically before proceeding through customs. Both solutions aim to enhance border security, streamline border processes, and improve the overall travel experience for passengers.</p></p>
