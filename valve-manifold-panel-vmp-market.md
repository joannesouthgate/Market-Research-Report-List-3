<p><p>Valve Manifold Panels (VMP) are commonly available in two market types - Automatic and Semi-automatic. In automatic VMPs, the valves are electronically controlled and operated, minimizing the need for manual intervention. This type of panel is ideal for applications requiring precision and consistency in valve control. On the other hand, semi-automatic VMPs require some level of manual operation alongside automated functions. They offer flexibility and customization options for users who prefer a more hands-on approach to valve control. Both types cater to different preferences and requirements in the market.</p></p>
