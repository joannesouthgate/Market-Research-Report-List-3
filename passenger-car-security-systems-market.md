<p><p>Passenger car security systems encompass various types to enhance safety and prevent theft. Immobilizers disable the engine, Remote keyless entry (RKE) allows users to lock and unlock doors remotely, Passive keyless entry (PKE) automatically unlocks doors when the key is in close proximity, and Passive keyless go (PKG) enables engine start without physically inserting the key. These systems provide convenience and security to vehicle owners, offering peace of mind and ensuring protection of their possessions.</p></p>
