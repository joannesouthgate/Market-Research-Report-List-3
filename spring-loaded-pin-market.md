<p><p>Spring-loaded pins are versatile electronic components used in a variety of applications. There are three main types of spring-loaded pin markets: surface mounted, through hole, and floating. Surface mounted pins are mounted directly onto the surface of a PCB. Through hole pins are inserted into holes on the PCB for secure connection. Floating pins allow for slight movement to accommodate misalignment. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the specific requirements of the application, providing flexibility and reliability in electronic connections.</p></p>
