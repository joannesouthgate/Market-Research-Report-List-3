<p><p>Linear Piezoelectric Motors are categorized into two main types - AC Piezoelectric Motors and DC Piezoelectric Motors. AC Piezoelectric Motors operate using alternating current, which causes the piezoelectric material to expand and contract in a specific pattern, creating linear motion. On the other hand, DC Piezoelectric Motors are powered by direct current, which generates linear motion through a similar expansion and contraction process. Both types offer precise and efficient linear motion capabilities, but their specific applications may vary based on the power source requirements.</p></p>
