<p><p>Cutting mats are available in various types, with two common options being hard-surface (self-sealing) mats and self-healing mats. Hard-surface mats are made of a durable material that is resistant to cutting marks, providing a sturdy surface for cutting and crafting. Self-healing mats are designed with a special material that closes up small cuts and marks made by cutting tools, ensuring a smooth surface even after repeated use. Both types of mats are popular choices for artists, crafters, and DIY enthusiasts for their durability and longevity.</p></p>
