<p><p>Chest Drainage Systems include traditional drain and mobile drain types. Traditional drains are fixed systems that connect to a patient's chest cavity to remove air or fluid build-up. They are typically used in hospital settings. Mobile drains, on the other hand, are portable devices that allow patients more mobility while still providing effective drainage. They are often used in home care settings or for patients who need to be more active during recovery. Both types play a crucial role in managing chest conditions and promoting patients' comfort and recovery.</p></p>
