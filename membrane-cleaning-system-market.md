<p><strong>The Membrane Cleaning System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flushing</li><li>Backwash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Membrane cleaning systems can be categorized into two main types: flushing and backwash. Flushing involves the use of a high flow of water or chemical solution to remove fouling and debris from the membrane surface. This process helps to maintain the efficiency and longevity of the membrane. On the other hand, backwash involves reversing the flow of water through the membrane to dislodge and remove contaminants. Both methods are essential for proper maintenance and upkeep of membrane filtration systems in various industries.</p></p>
