<p><ul><li>2 Pipes System</li><li>4 Pipes System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multi-function heat pumps are available in two main types: 2 pipes system and 4 pipes system. A 2 pipes system uses the same pipes for heating and cooling, meaning that the system can only heat or cool at one time. On the other hand, a 4 pipes system uses separate pipes for heating and cooling, allowing for simultaneous heating and cooling in different areas of a building. Both systems offer efficient temperature control and energy savings, catering to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
