<p><p>Wired bioimpedance devices are categorized into two main types: Single-frequency bioimpedance devices and Multiple-frequency bioimpedance devices. Single-frequency devices operate at a fixed frequency and are suitable for basic impedance measurements. On the other hand, multiple-frequency bioimpedance devices can measure impedance at various frequencies, providing more detailed information about the body composition. Both types of devices are used in various applications such as monitoring body composition, assessing hydration levels, and tracking changes in health and fitness.</p></p>
