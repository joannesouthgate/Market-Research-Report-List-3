<p><p>Laundry cleaning detergents market types include anionic detergents, cationic detergents, non-ionic detergents, and zwitterionic detergents. Anionic detergents are effective in removing dirt and oil stains due to their negative charge. Cationic detergents are good at disinfecting and removing bacteria but may not be suitable for all fabrics. Non-ionic detergents are gentle on fabrics and are often used in laundry products for sensitive skin. Zwitterionic detergents are versatile and can work well in both hard and soft water conditions.</p></p>
