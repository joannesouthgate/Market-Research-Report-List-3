<p><strong>The Auto Grease Lube System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct System</li><li>Indirect System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Auto Grease Lube System market consists of two main types: Direct System and Indirect System. The Direct System delivers grease directly to the friction points through individual lines, ensuring precise lubrication. On the other hand, the Indirect System delivers grease to a central distribution block which then distributes it to various lubrication points through interconnected lines. Each system has its own advantages and is used based on the specific needs and requirements of the machinery or equipment being lubricated.</p></p>
