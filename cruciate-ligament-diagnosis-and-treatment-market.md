<p><p>Cruciate Ligament Diagnosis and Treatment Market includes two main types: Posterior Cruciate Ligament (PCL) and Anterior Cruciate Ligament (ACL). PCL injuries are less common than ACL injuries but can still cause significant knee instability. Diagnosis often involves physical examinations and imaging tests. Treatment may include rest, physical therapy, or surgery. ACL injuries are more common and often occur during sports or activities that involve sudden stops or changes in direction. Treatment may involve surgery followed by rehabilitation to restore strength and stability to the knee joint.</p></p>
