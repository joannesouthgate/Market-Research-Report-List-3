<p><p>CCD industrial cameras are widely used in various industries for machine vision applications. The two main types of CCD industrial cameras are area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras are ideal for capturing still images of objects within a specific area, while line scan cameras are better suited for capturing continuous images of moving objects or objects on a conveyor belt. Both types of cameras offer high-quality imaging capabilities and play a crucial role in quality control and inspection processes in manufacturing and other industries.</p></p>
