<p><p>Axial grain dryers can be categorized into two main types: single plenum and split plenum. Single plenum dryers have a single chamber where the grain is dried uniformly, while split plenum dryers have separate chambers for drying different sections of the grain. Single plenum dryers are more commonly used for smaller operations, while split plenum dryers are preferred for larger operations where different grains or batches require varying drying conditions. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the user.</p></p>
