<p><p>Brass solenoid valves are commonly categorized into two types: Normally Open and Normally Closed. Normally Open valves are designed to stay open when no power is applied, closing only when an electric current is supplied. On the other hand, Normally Closed valves remain closed without power and only open when energized. These distinctions allow for versatile applications in various industries, providing precise control over the flow of liquids or gases. Both types offer reliable performance and are essential components in fluid control systems.</p></p>
