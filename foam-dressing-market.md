<p><p>Foam dressings in the medical field come in two main types: adhesive foam dressings and non-adhesive foam dressings. Adhesive foam dressings have an adhesive border that attaches securely to the skin surrounding a wound, providing a barrier against contamination and helping to maintain a moist wound environment. Non-adhesive foam dressings do not have an adhesive border and are generally held in place with tape or a bandage. Both types of foam dressings are designed to promote healing and provide a comfortable fit for patients.</p></p>
