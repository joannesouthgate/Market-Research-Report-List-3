<p><p>Two-wheeler starter motors are essential components that help initiate the engine in a motorcycle or scooter. There are three main types of starter motors used in the market - electric starter motors, pneumatic starter motors, and hydraulic starter motors. Electric starter motors are commonly used in most two-wheelers due to their reliability and efficiency. Pneumatic starter motors utilize compressed air to start the engine, while hydraulic starter motors use fluid pressure for the same purpose. Each type of starter motor offers unique advantages and is chosen based on specific requirements.</p></p>
