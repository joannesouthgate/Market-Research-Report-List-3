<p><p>Reversible thermochromic pigment can be classified into two main types based on their composition: organic and inorganic. Organic thermochromic pigments are composed of carbon-based molecules and are typically more environmentally friendly and offer a wider range of color options. In contrast, inorganic thermochromic pigments are made of metal oxides and tend to be more stable and durable. Both types of pigments change color in response to temperature fluctuations, making them popular in various applications such as textiles, packaging, and security printing.</p></p>
