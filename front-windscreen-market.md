<p><p>Front windscreen market includes two main types of glass - laminated glass and tempered glass. Laminated glass is made by bonding two or more layers of glass with a layer of plastic in between, offering increased safety as it doesn't shatter upon impact. Tempered glass is processed by rapid heating and cooling to increase its strength, making it harder to break but if it does, it shatters into small, dull pieces. Both types of glass cater to different needs in the automotive industry, providing varying levels of safety and protection.</p></p>
