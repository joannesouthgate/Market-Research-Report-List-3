<p><p>Pipe and thread sealants are commonly used in plumbing and construction applications to prevent leakage and provide a secure seal. The market includes various types such as PTFE tape, Haste & Paste sealants, sealing cords, and others. PTFE tape is a thin, flexible tape that is wrapped around pipe threads to create a tight seal. Haste & Paste sealants are applied directly to the threads to fill in any gaps and provide a stronger seal. Sealing cords are pre-formed cords that are used to seal larger gaps or joints.</p></p>
