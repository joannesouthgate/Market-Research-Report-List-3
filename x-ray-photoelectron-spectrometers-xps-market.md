<p><p>X-ray Photoelectron Spectrometers (XPS) market types can be classified into two categories: Monochromatic and Non-monochromatic. Monochromatic XPS systems use a single energy X-ray source, resulting in high resolution spectra and precise analysis of chemical elements. On the other hand, Non-monochromatic XPS systems utilize multiple energy X-ray sources, providing a wider range of analysis capabilities but with slightly lower resolution. Both types of XPS systems have their own advantages and are used in various industries for material analysis and research purposes.</p></p>
