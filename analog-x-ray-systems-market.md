<p><ul><li>Film Based</li><li>CR Film Cassette</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Analog X-ray systems have two main market types, which are film based systems and CR film cassette systems. Film based systems use traditional photographic film to capture x-ray images, which are then developed in a darkroom. CR film cassette systems use a cassette containing a photostimulable phosphor plate that captures x-ray images, which are then processed and viewed on a computer. Both systems are widely used in healthcare facilities for diagnostic imaging purposes.</p></p>
