<p><strong>The Distribution Transformers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Phase</li><li>Three-Phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distribution transformers are classified into single-phase and three-phase types based on the number of primary and secondary windings. Single-phase transformers have one primary and one secondary winding, suitable for small residential applications. Three-phase transformers have three primary and three secondary windings, commonly used in industrial and commercial settings where higher power distribution is required. Both types play a crucial role in stepping down voltage levels for efficient power distribution across different sectors of the economy.</p></p>
