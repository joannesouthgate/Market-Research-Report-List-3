<p><strong>The Contact Cardiac Mapping System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electroanatomical mapping</li><li>Basket catheter mapping</li><li>Traditional endocardial catheter mapping</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Contact Cardiac Mapping Systems are medical devices used to pinpoint the source of cardiac arrhythmias in patients. There are three main types of mapping systems available - Electroanatomical mapping, which creates a 3D map of the heart's electrical activity; Basket catheter mapping, which uses a basket-shaped catheter to collect electrical signals from inside the heart chambers; and Traditional endocardial catheter mapping, which involves threading a catheter through the blood vessels to record electrical signals from within the heart. Each type offers unique benefits for diagnosing and treating cardiac arrhythmias.</p></p>
