<p><p>Surgical instrument tracking software utilizes two main technologies for tracking purposes: RFID and barcode. RFID technology uses radio waves to identify and track instruments, providing real-time updates on their location and status. Barcode technology involves scanning printed codes on instruments to achieve the same tracking capabilities. Both technologies offer efficient and accurate inventory management, reduce errors, and improve patient safety. Healthcare facilities can choose between RFID and barcode solutions based on their specific needs and budget constraints.</p></p>
