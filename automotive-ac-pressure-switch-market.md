<p><p>The Automotive AC Pressure Switch Market consists of various types including the Dual Function Pressure Switch, HPCO (High-Pressure Cut-Out) Switch, Fan Switch, and Compressor Cycling Switch. The Dual Function Pressure Switch is designed to monitor both high and low pressure levels in the AC system. The HPCO Switch is responsible for shutting off the compressor in case of high pressure. The Fan Switch activates the cooling fans of the system. The Compressor Cycling Switch regulates the operation of the compressor to maintain optimal pressure levels.</p></p>
