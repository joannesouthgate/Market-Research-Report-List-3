<p><p>Renal dialysis equipment market includes two main types of dialysis treatment: hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis. Hemodialysis involves filtering a patient's blood through a machine to remove waste products and excess fluids, while peritoneal dialysis uses the lining of the patient's abdomen to filter waste and excess fluids out of the body. Both types of dialysis treatment require specialized equipment such as dialysis machines, catheters, and solution bags, contributing to the growth of the renal dialysis equipment market.</p></p>
