<p><p>FLNG Market Types refer to the different methods of liquefying natural gas offshore for transportation. LNG FPSO involves converting a floating production, storage, and offloading vessel into a LNG processing facility. FSRU, on the other hand, stands for Floating Storage and Regasification Unit, which is used to store and regasify LNG before delivering it to onshore facilities. Both technologies offer advantages in terms of flexibility, cost-effectiveness, and quicker project implementation compared to traditional onshore LNG plants.</p></p>
