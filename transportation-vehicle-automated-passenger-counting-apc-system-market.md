<p><p>Automated Passenger Counting (APC) systems for transportation vehicles come in various types such as Infrared Type, Time-of-Flight Type, and Stereoscopic Vision Type. Infrared Type systems use infrared sensors to detect passengers entering or exiting the vehicle. Time-of-Flight Type systems measure the time taken for light to travel from the sensor to the object and back again. Stereoscopic Vision Type systems use dual cameras to create a 3D image for accurate passenger counting. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of accuracy and reliability.</p></p>
