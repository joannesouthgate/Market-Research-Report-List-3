<p><p>Press molding machines are used for shaping materials by applying pressure and heat. Hot press molding machines use heat to soften the material before applying pressure, which helps in creating intricate shapes and designs. Cold press molding machines, on the other hand, do not use heat and are primarily used for simple shaping processes. Both types of machines are widely used in industries such as woodworking, automotive, and aerospace for manufacturing a variety of products.</p></p>
