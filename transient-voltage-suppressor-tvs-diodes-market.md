There are two main types of TVS diodes: Uni-polar TVS and Bi-polar TVS. Uni-polar TVS diodes are designed to protect against either positive or negative voltage spikes, while Bi-polar TVS diodes can protect against both positive and negative voltage spikes. The market for Uni-polar TVS diodes is driven by their cost effectiveness and efficiency in protecting against specific voltage spikes, while the Bi-polar TVS market caters to applications requiring protection against both positive and negative voltage spikes.</p></p>
