Non-directional Beacons provide non-directional navigation signals, while ILS assists in precision landing approaches. DME measures the slant range distance from an aircraft to a ground station. DVOR provides pilots with directional information. Inertial Navigation System relies on gyroscopes and accelerometers to determine aircraft position. Other navigation aids include GPS and radar systems.</p></p>
