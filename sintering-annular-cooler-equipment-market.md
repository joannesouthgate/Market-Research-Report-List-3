<p><p>Sintering annular cooler equipment market is divided into three main types: transmission device, rotary device, and feed & discharge hopper. The transmission device is responsible for moving materials through the cooler efficiently. The rotary device helps evenly distribute heat and cool the materials inside the cooler. The feed & discharge hopper manage the input and output of materials into and out of the cooler, ensuring a smooth and continuous process. These components are essential for the optimal functioning of sintering annular cooler equipment.</p></p>
