Cell counters are used to quantify and analyze cell populations in a sample, facilitating research in cell biology and bioprocessing. Other equipment in the market may include bioreactors, spectrophotometers, and chromatography systems, all essential for analyzing and optimizing bioprocessing techniques.</p></p>
