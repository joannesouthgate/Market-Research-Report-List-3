<p><p>Watertight doors and windows are essential components in marine and offshore structures to prevent water ingress. There are two main types in the market: hinged watertight doors and windows, and sliding watertight doors and windows. Hinged watertight doors and windows open on a hinge like traditional doors, providing a secure seal when closed. Sliding watertight doors and windows, on the other hand, operate by sliding horizontally to open and close, offering a space-saving solution while maintaining watertight integrity.</p></p>
