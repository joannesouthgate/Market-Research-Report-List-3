<p><strong>The Auto Lensometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Visual Type Lensmeter</li><li>Projection Type Lensmeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Auto lensometers are available in two main types: visual type lensmeters and projection type lensmeters. </p><p>Visual type lensmeters require the user to view the lens and focus manually, while projection type lensmeters project a pattern onto the lens and analyze the reflected light to measure the lens power. </p><p>Visual type lensmeters are commonly used in smaller optical practices, while projection type lensmeters are preferred in larger practices or clinics for their speed and accuracy in measuring lens power.</p></p>
