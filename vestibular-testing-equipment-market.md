<p><strong>The Vestibular Testing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Videonystagmography (VNG) System</li><li>Vestibular Evoked Myogenic Potential (VEMP) System</li><li>Rotary Chair</li><li>Computerized Dynamic Posturography</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Videonystagmography (VNG) System is used to evaluate the vestibular and ocular motor function by measuring the movements of the eyes during different visual and vestibular stimuli. Vestibular Evoked Myogenic Potential (VEMP) System is used to assess the function of the otolithic organs in the inner ear. Rotary Chair is used to evaluate the vestibulo-ocular reflexes by rotating the patient at different speeds. Computerized Dynamic Posturography assesses balance and sensory function. Other types of vestibular testing equipment include caloric testing devices and vestibular autorotation testing systems.</p></p>
