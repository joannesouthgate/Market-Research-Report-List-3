<p><p>Semiconductor handling robots are categorized into vacuum robotic and atmospheric robotic markets based on the environment in which they operate. Vacuum robotic systems are designed for high precision handling in vacuum environments to prevent contamination and ensure product quality. On the other hand, atmospheric robotic systems are used in ambient air environments for tasks such as pick-and-place operations. Both types of robots play a crucial role in the semiconductor industry by automating processes and improving efficiency.</p></p>
