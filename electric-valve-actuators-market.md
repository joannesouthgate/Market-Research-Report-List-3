<p><strong>The Electric Valve Actuators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight Stroke Electric Actuators</li><li>Multi-turn Electric Actuators</li><li>Partial Rotary Electric Actuators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric valve actuators are categorized into three main types: Straight Stroke Electric Actuators, which operate in a linear motion to control valves with a direct push or pull mechanism; Multi-turn Electric Actuators, which rotate multiple times to open or close valves over a longer distance; and Partial Rotary Electric Actuators, which provide a limited rotation to control valves in a specific angle or range. Each of these actuator types serves a unique purpose in the market based on the specific valve control requirements.</p></p>
