<p><p>Digital Audio Broadcasting (DAB) Market Types- III Band, L Band refer to different frequency bands used for broadcasting digital audio signals. III Band operates between 174-230 MHz, providing a wider coverage area and higher capacity for broadcasting. L Band, on the other hand, operates between 1452-1492 MHz and is suitable for more localized broadcasting with lower power requirements. Both market types cater to different broadcasting needs, with III Band favored for national coverage and L Band for local coverage.</p></p>
