<p><p>The residential air purification equipment market consists of several types, including High-efficiency Particulate Air (HEPA) filters, Electrostatic Precipitators (ESPs), Ionizers, and Ozone Generators. HEPA filters are known for their ability to capture small particles like pollen and dust. ESPs use an electric charge to remove particles from the air. Ionizers release charged particles that attract and remove contaminants. Ozone Generators produce ozone to eliminate odors and kill bacteria. Each type of equipment offers unique benefits for improving indoor air quality.</p></p>
