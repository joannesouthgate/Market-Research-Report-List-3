<p><p>Semiconductor Thermal Evaporators market can be divided into two main types: Single Effect Evaporation and Multi Effect Evaporation. Single Effect Evaporation involves the evaporation of liquid semiconductor materials using a single heating source, while Multi Effect Evaporation utilizes multiple heating sources to evaporate the material more efficiently. Single Effect Evaporation is simpler and more cost-effective, while Multi Effect Evaporation offers higher productivity and energy savings. Both types play a crucial role in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
