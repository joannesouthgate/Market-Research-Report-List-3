<p><p>Automotive pressure sensors are crucial components in vehicles, with various types serving different functions. ABS (Anti-lock Braking System) sensors monitor pressure in the brake system to prevent skidding. Airbag sensors detect impact and trigger airbag deployment. TPMS (Tire Pressure Monitoring System) sensors monitor tire pressure for safety and efficiency. Engine sensors measure intake manifold pressure for optimal engine performance. HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning) sensors regulate pressure in the climate control system. Transmission sensors monitor pressure for smooth gear shifting.</p></p>
