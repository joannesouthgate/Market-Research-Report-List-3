<p><p>Thermography Camera (TIC) market consists of two main types - Cooled TIC and Uncooled TIC. Cooled TICs use a cryogenic cooler to maintain a low temperature inside the camera, resulting in higher image resolution and sensitivity, making them ideal for demanding applications. Uncooled TICs, on the other hand, do not require a cooling system, making them more cost-effective and suitable for general use. Both types of TICs have their own advantages and are used in various industries like firefighting, industrial inspections, and security.</p></p>
