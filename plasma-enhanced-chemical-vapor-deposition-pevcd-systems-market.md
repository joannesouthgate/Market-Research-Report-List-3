<p><p>Plasma Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition (PEVCD) Systems are used for depositing thin films on substrates in industries like microelectronics and photonics. The two main types of PEVCD systems are Parallel Plate Type PECVD Systems and Tube Type PECVD Systems. Parallel Plate systems use electrodes to generate plasma between two parallel plates, while Tube Type systems utilize a cylindrical chamber for plasma generation. Both systems offer efficient deposition processes for various materials and applications in the semiconductor and optoelectronics industries.</p></p>
