<p><p>Small fiber collimators are used to convert the divergent light emitted by small core fibers into a parallel beam. There are three main types of small fiber collimators: Single Mode Fiber, Polarization Maintaining Fiber, and Multimode Fiber. </p><p>- Single Mode Fiber collimators are designed for use with single mode fibers, ensuring the transmission of a single mode of light. </p><p>- Polarization Maintaining Fiber collimators maintain the polarization state of light passing through the collimator, ideal for applications requiring precise control over light polarization.</p></p>
