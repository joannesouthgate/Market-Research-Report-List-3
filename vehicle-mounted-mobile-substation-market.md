<p><p>Vehicle-mounted Mobile Substation market includes HV, LV, and MV Mobile Substations. HV Mobile Substations are designed for high voltage applications, typically above 100 kV, and are used for power distribution in areas with high demand. LV Mobile Substations operate at low voltages, usually below 1 kV, and are suitable for providing temporary power in construction sites or emergency situations. MV Mobile Substations fall between HV and LV, with voltage levels ranging from 1 kV to 100 kV, serving as an intermediate power distribution solution.</p></p>
