<p><ul><li>Behind-the-ear (BTE) Hearing Aids</li><li>In-the-ear (ITE) Hearing Aids</li><li>In-The-Canal (ITC) Hearing Aids</li><li>Completely-In-Canal (CIC) Hearing Aids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hearing aids for adults come in different types to suit individual needs. Behind-the-ear (BTE) hearing aids are worn behind the ear, while In-the-ear (ITE) hearing aids fit inside the ear. In-The-Canal (ITC) hearing aids are smaller and fit partially inside the ear canal, while Completely-In-Canal (CIC) hearing aids are the smallest type and fit entirely inside the ear canal. Each type offers different levels of comfort, discretion, and effectiveness in amplifying sound for individuals with hearing loss.</p></p>
