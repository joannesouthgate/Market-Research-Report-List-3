Microphones are components that capture sound and convert it into electrical signals. Speakers play the role of converting electrical signals back into sound. Receivers are responsible for converting electrical signals into sound and transmitting them to the user's ear. These three types of components are essential for the functioning of audio features in smartphones.</p></p>
