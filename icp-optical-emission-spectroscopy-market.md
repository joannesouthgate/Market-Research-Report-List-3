<p><p>ICP Optical Emission Spectroscopy market consists of two main types: Sequential Type and Simultaneous Type. Sequential Type operates by analyzing one element at a time, making it slower but offering higher sensitivity and accuracy. Simultaneous Type, on the other hand, can analyze multiple elements simultaneously, providing faster results but with lower sensitivity. Both types cater to different customer needs and are widely used in various industries for elemental analysis and quality control purposes.</p></p>
