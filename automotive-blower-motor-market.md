<p><p>Automotive blower motors are crucial components in vehicles that help regulate airflow for heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems. There are two main types of motors used in automotive blower systems: brushed motors and brushless motors. Brushed motors have an internal commutator and brushes that transfer electrical power to the rotor, while brushless motors use electronic commutation instead. Brushless motors are more efficient, reliable, and durable than brushed motors, making them a popular choice in modern automotive applications.</p></p>
