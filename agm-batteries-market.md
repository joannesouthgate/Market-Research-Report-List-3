<p><p>AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) Batteries are sealed lead-acid batteries that use a fiberglass mat to absorb and trap the electrolyte solution between the battery plates. This design makes AGM batteries spill-proof, maintenance-free, and capable of delivering high currents on demand. AGM batteries are commonly used in applications such as automotive, marine, renewable energy, and UPS systems.</p><p>The AGM Batteries Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.5% during the forecast period.
