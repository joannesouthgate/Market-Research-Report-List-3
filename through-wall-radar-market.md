<p><p>Through-wall radar technology has evolved to include various market types, namely 1D, 2D, and 3D systems. 1D radar provides linear information about targets behind walls, such as distance and approximate size. 2D radar offers additional details by providing a two-dimensional image of the target's shape and movement. On the other hand, 3D radar offers a more comprehensive view by displaying detailed three-dimensional images of targets, allowing for accurate reconstructions of their surroundings. Each market type serves different applications and offers varying levels of information for users.</p></p>
