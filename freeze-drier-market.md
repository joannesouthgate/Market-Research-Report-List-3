<p><p>A freeze drier, also known as a lyophilizer, is a machine that removes moisture from biological samples such as food, pharmaceuticals, and chemical materials by freezing them and then subjecting them to a vacuum environment to sublimate the frozen water. This process preserves the samples and increases their shelf life by preventing microbial growth and chemical reactions.</p><p>The freeze drier market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.4% during the forecast period. The increasing demand for freeze-dried food products due to their longer shelf life and retention of nutrients and flavor is a major driver for market growth. Manufacturers are focusing on introducing automated systems with enhanced control and monitoring capabilities to streamline the freeze-drying process and reduce human intervention. Additionally, the growing popularity of freeze-dried pet foods and snacks is creating new opportunities for market growth.</p></p>
