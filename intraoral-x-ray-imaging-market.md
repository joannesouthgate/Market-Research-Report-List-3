<p><strong>The Intraoral X-ray Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bitewing X-rays</li><li>Periapical X-rays</li><li>Occlusal X-rays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intraoral X-ray Imaging Market includes three main types of imaging techniques: Bitewing X-rays, Periapical X-rays, and Occlusal X-rays. Bitewing X-rays focus on capturing images of the upper and lower teeth in a single view to detect cavities and monitor bone levels. Periapical X-rays provide detailed images of the entire tooth and surrounding bone to diagnose dental issues such as infections and abscesses. Occlusal X-rays offer a broader view of the floor of the mouth to examine the placement and development of teeth.</p></p>
