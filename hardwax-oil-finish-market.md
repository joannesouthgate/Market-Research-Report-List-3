<p><p>Hardwax Oil Finish is available in two main market types: Single-Component and Two-Component. Single-Component Hardwax Oil is a ready-to-use product that combines oil and wax in one solution, making it convenient for quick application. On the other hand, Two-Component Hardwax Oil consists of separate oil and wax components that need to be mixed together before application for a more customizable finish. Both options offer a durable and protective finish for wood surfaces, with Two-Component allowing for more control over the final result.</p></p>
