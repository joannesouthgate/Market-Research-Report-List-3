<p><p>Electrodeionization market offers two main types of constructions: Plate and Frame Construction and Spiral Wound Construction. Plate and Frame Construction involves electrodes separated by gaskets in a frame, allowing for easy disassembly and maintenance. Spiral Wound Construction features spiral-wound modules with alternating layers of ion exchange membranes and electrodes, providing a compact and efficient design. Both types are widely used in industries such as pharmaceuticals, power generation, and electronics for removing ions and impurities from water through an electric field.</p></p>
