<p><p>Industrial equipment assembly market types can be categorized as full-automatic and semi-automatic. In a full-automatic market, the assembly process is completely automated, requiring minimal human intervention. This results in higher efficiency, faster production rates, and lower labor costs. On the other hand, in a semi-automatic market, the assembly process involves a combination of automated machinery and manual labor. This allows for greater flexibility and customization in the assembly process, but may result in slightly slower production rates compared to full-automatic systems.</p></p>
