<p><p>Advanced Process Control (APC) refers to a range of technologies and strategies used to optimize industrial processes. Advanced Regulatory Control focuses on optimizing individual loops within a process. Multivariable Model Predictive Control uses mathematical models to optimize multiple variables simultaneously. Inferential Control uses inferred variables to improve process efficiency. Sequential Control manages the sequence of operations in a process. Compressor Control specifically focuses on optimizing the operation of compressors within a process. These technologies play a crucial role in improving production efficiency and reducing costs in various industries.</p></p>
