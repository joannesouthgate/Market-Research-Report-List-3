<p><strong>The Monochrome Microscope Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SCCD Camera</li><li>SCMOS Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Monochrome microscope cameras are available in two main types - SCCD camera and sCMOS camera. SCCD cameras utilize charge-coupled device technology to capture high-quality images with excellent sensitivity and low noise levels. On the other hand, sCMOS cameras use scientific complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology to provide high-resolution images with fast frame rates, making them ideal for demanding research applications. Both types offer unique benefits and are popular choices among researchers and professionals in the field of microscopy.</p></p>
