<p><p>Variable Air Volume (VAV) operating systems come in various types to cater to different building requirements. Single-duct VAV systems deliver a single temperature air stream to spaces. Dual-duct VAV systems use two air streams with different temperatures to meet varying heating and cooling demands. Induction VAV systems utilize the Venturi effect to mix primary and secondary air for temperature control. Fan-powered VAV systems use fans to boost airflow in spaces with high static pressure. Each type offers unique advantages for energy efficiency and comfort control in commercial buildings.</p></p>
