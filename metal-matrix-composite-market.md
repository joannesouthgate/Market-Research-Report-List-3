<p><p>Metal Matrix Composites (MMCs) are a type of material made by embedding reinforcing fibers into a metal matrix. Two common types of MMCs are Aluminum Metal Matrix Composites (Al-MMCs) and Magnesium Metal Matrix Composites (Mg-MMCs). Al-MMCs are known for their high strength-to-weight ratio and excellent thermal conductivity, making them popular in aerospace and automotive industries. Mg-MMCs, on the other hand, are lightweight and offer good damping properties, making them suitable for applications where weight reduction and vibration control are important.</p></p>
