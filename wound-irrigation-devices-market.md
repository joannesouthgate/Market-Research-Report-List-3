<p><p>Wound irrigation devices are used to clean and irrigate wounds during medical procedures. There are several types of wound irrigation devices available including bulb syringes, piston syringes, pressure canisters, whirlpool agitators, whirlpool hose sprayers, and irrigation fluid. Bulb syringes and piston syringes are handheld devices that deliver a controlled stream of irrigation fluid. Pressure canisters use compressed air to deliver irrigation fluid. Whirlpool agitators and hose sprayers are designed for larger wounds and provide a more continuous flow of irrigation fluid.</p></p>
