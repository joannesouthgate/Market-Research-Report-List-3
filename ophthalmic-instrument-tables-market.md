<p><p>Ophthalmic instrument tables differ in types based on their operation: manual type and electric type. Manual tables are operated through hand-crank mechanisms, offering flexibility in height adjustment. They are cost-effective and suitable for smaller clinics or practices. On the other hand, electric tables feature motorized systems for height adjustment, providing convenience and efficiency. These tables are preferred in larger healthcare facilities due to their advanced functionalities. Both types cater to the needs of ophthalmologists and optometrists in their daily practice.</p></p>
