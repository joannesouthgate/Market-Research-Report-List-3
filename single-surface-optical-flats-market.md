<p><strong>The Single Surface Optical Flats Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Accuracy λ/4</li><li>Accuracy λ/10</li><li>Accuracy λ/20</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single surface optical flats are categorized based on their accuracy levels, such as λ/4, λ/10, λ/20, and others. The accuracy level refers to the flatness deviation of the surface from a perfect plane, with lower values indicating higher precision. For example, a flat with λ/4 accuracy means that surface irregularities are within one quarter of a wavelength of light. Higher accuracy levels are typically required for more precise measurements in industries like optics, aerospace, and semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
